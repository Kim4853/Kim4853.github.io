    <h2>Biography</h2>

    <p>
      BongSeok Kim is a Ph.D. student in the School of Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University, advised by Professors Guang Lin and Li Qiao. His research lies at the intersection of scientific machine learning, computational science, uncertainty quantification, and computational mechanics.
    </p>

    <p>
      His research focuses on developing mathematically grounded machine learning methodologies for computational science by integrating numerical analysis, physical principles, and data-driven modeling. Current research interests include scientific machine learning, machine learning for partial differential equations, data-driven physics discovery, operator learning, uncertainty quantification, and digital twins for engineering systems.
    </p>
